We are a team of four students from Davidson College, whose mission is to empower fitness enthusiasts and athletes by providing an intelligent, computer vision-powered platform that delivers precise and actionable feedback on workout performance. VisionFit analyzes video input to assess barbell speed, velocity, form, fatigue, and injury risk, with a dedicated focus on the three fundamental lifts: squat, bench, and deadlift. Beyond movement analysis, VisionFit offers personalized diet recommendations, ensuring a comprehensive approach to performance optimization and injury prevention. Additionally, we aim to enhance trainer-athlete collaboration by enabling trainers to review and refine their athletes' performances, fostering continuous improvement and excellence.
