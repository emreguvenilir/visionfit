Understanding the Metrics

VisionFit calculates speed, power, and control across each repetition using frame-by-frame analysis. Average speed reflects overall pacing, while max speed captures peak explosiveness. Consistency indicates steadiness across reps, and fatigue index measures change in performance over time. Time under tension represents total duration of active movement, helping gauge muscle endurance.

How To Use the Dashboard

Welcome to VisionFit — your intelligent performance tracker. Currently, the system supports side-view squat analysis using our computer-vision model.

  • Upload a clear side-angle squat video (ideally 3–10 seconds, good lighting, full body visible).
  • Enter your height, body weight, lift weight, RPE, and reps before submitting.
  • Our vision model will automatically track barbell motion, calculate velocity, fatigue, power, and consistency, and generate lift feedback using Gemini AI.
  • Once complete, you’ll see your results, velocity graph, and AI feedback in the dashboard.
  • For best accuracy, keep the camera steady and ensure your full movement stays in frame.

Stay tuned — support for front-view squats, deadlifts, and bench presses is coming soon!
